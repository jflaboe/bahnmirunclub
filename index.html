About Us

Welcome to the Bahn Mi Run Club! We're a community of runners who believe that the best part of any run is the delicious Vietnamese sandwich waiting at the end.

Whether you're a seasoned marathoner or just starting your running journey, our club welcomes all paces and all appetites. We explore local trails, discover new bahn mi spots, and build lasting friendships along the way.

Join us for weekly runs followed by group meals at the best Vietnamese restaurants in town. Because every good run deserves a great bahn mi!

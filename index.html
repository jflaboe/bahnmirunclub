About Us

Welcome to the Bahn Mi Run Club! We're a community of runners who believe that the best part of any run is the delicious Vietnamese sandwich waiting at the end.

Whether you're a seasoned marathoner or just starting your running journey, our club welcomes all paces and all appetites.

Join us for bi-weekly runs followed by group meals at the best Vietnamese restaurants in town. Because every good run deserves a great bahn mi!
